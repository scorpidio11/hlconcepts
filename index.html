Throughout the ages, food continues to bring us together. Developed by serial restaurateur & master chef Hyunwook Lee, our mission is to make high quality, authentic Asian cuisine accessible to all.

Arizona's first and only Omakase room – by Shinji Kurita

In Japanese, Omakase is roughly translated as "I'll leave it up to you." Shinbay is not just a restaurant; it is a carefully curated dining experience, led by James Beard semifinalist Chef Shinji Kurita. Experience an unforgettable and intimate evening of exotic flavors and dazzling artistry, on your plate.

Masterfully crafted, authentic Korean-Chinese cuisine.

Arizona's first and only premiere Korean-Chinese dining experience. Born from generations of Chinese immigrants living in Korea, authentic traditional Chinese recipes have been adapted to the Korean palate, including unique sauces, flavors, and cooking methods. Dressed in a luxurious and sumptuous setting and a high-class bar and lounge, Jin Jia offers an elevated and memorable dining experience unlike any other.

Authentic Korean Barbecue – Prime cuts only.

Authentic Korean barbecue with a focus on the freshest Prime meats and ingredients, all grilled right at your table. Gather friends and family for a new and adventurous dining experience ensuring endless unique flavors and centuries of classic Korean culinary techniques.

Meet Our Team

Founder

Hyunwook Lee

Founder & Executive Chef

Lee was born with a passion for both structural and edible design. He is versed in many styles of dining, with heavy emphasis on Korean, Japanese and Asian Fusion cuisines.

With a passion for sharing the highest quality authentic Asian cuisine with the world, Lee is driven to deliver only the best-in-class at each of his restaurants.

Having opened six locations of his dining experiences, Lee is at the top of his class when it comes to ambition, and slowing down is not in his forecast.
